Project Overview

The Public Transport Route Planner helps users find the best public transport routes — including bus, metro, and train — between any source and destination within supported Indian metro cities.

🔁 Best recommended route
📍 Alternative available routes
⏱ Estimated travel time

Key Features

Route optimization with best & alternative routes
Time and cost estimation for each route
Bus, metro & railway transport modes
Accessibility info — ramps, elevators
Viewpoints discovery between source & destination
Multi-modal journey planning

♿ Accessibility Focus

We believe public transport should be accessible to everyone. The app highlights whether stations and vehicles offer ramps, lifts, and other facilities for persons with disabilities.

👁 Viewpoints Discovery

Travelling somewhere new? Discover tourist attractions, scenic spots, and notable landmarks along your route.
